Interaction Rules

Click ● on parent

Hides parent + all children. Unhiding parent restores children to their previous state (independently hidden children stay hidden).

Click ● on leaf

Toggles just that mesh. Parent stays visible. In the 3D view, only that specific mesh disappears.

Double-click node label

Focus camera on this node (existing behavior, preserved).

Design layers

Bracket, coupler, clearance, insertion channel — appear as children of their component. Hidden by default, toggle on to inspect design.

A

Single ● / ○ toggle (Fusion 360 style)

One circle per node. Click to toggle. No separate isolate icon. Simple and direct.

B

● / ○ toggle + right-click "Solo"

Same toggle, but right-click (or alt-click) a node to "solo" it — hide everything else. Click again to un-solo.
